# How to Add a New Coin

This guide shows you how to add a coin to your collection using the Scriptorium form.

## Prerequisites

- Patina is running and displaying the Cabinet (gallery view)
- You have a coin ready to catalog

## Steps

1. **Navigate to the Scriptorium**:
   Click the **+ New Entry** button in the header toolbar.
   The Scriptorium form opens with a dual-folio layout.

2. **Fill in the Designation**:
   Enter a title for the coin in the first field (e.g., "Antoninianus of Gordian III").

3. **Capture Images** (optional):
   Click **Establish Wireless Bridge** in the Plate Editor to open the Lens.
   Scan the QR code with your mobile device to capture and transfer images.
   Alternatively, click the image slots to add existing images.

4. **Complete the Ledger Folio**:
   Fill in the numismatic details:
   - **Issuer**: The ruler or authority (e.g., "Gordian III")
   - **Denomination**: The coin type (e.g., "Antoninianus")
   - **Year**: Display and numeric year
   - **Era**: Ancient, Medieval, or Modern
   - **Mint**: Minting location (e.g., "Rome")
   - **Material**: Metal composition (e.g., "Silver")
   - **Weight**: Weight in grams (2 decimal places)
   - **Diameter**: Diameter in millimeters
   - **Die Axis**: Position in hours (e.g., "6h")
   - **Grade**: Condition grade

5. **Add Descriptions**:
   - **Obverse Legend**: Text on the front
   - **Obverse Description**: Visual description
   - **Reverse Legend**: Text on the back
   - **Reverse Description**: Visual description
   - **Edge**: Edge markings (e.g., "Reeded")

6. **Record Provenance** (optional):
   - **Catalog Reference**: RIC, RPC, or Crawford number
   - **Provenance**: Previous ownership history
   - **Curator's Note**: Your observations or story about the coin

7. **Save the Entry**:
   Click **Index to Ledger** at the bottom of the form.
   Patina validates your entries and saves the coin to your archive.

## Auto-Draft

Patina automatically saves your progress every 2 seconds to prevent data loss.
If you close the form before saving, your draft is preserved in local storage.

## Troubleshooting

- **Validation Error**: Ensure weight uses 2 decimal places (e.g., "3.25")
- **Missing Required Field**: The "title" field is required
- **Image Upload Failed**: Verify your mobile device is on the same network as the desktop
