# How to Use Lens Wireless Bridge

This guide shows you how to capture coin images wirelessly from your mobile device to your desktop.

## Prerequisites

- Patina desktop application is running
- Your mobile device is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your desktop

## Steps

1. **Start a Lens Session**:
   Open the Scriptorium (Add/Edit form) or click the Lens icon.
   Click **Establish Wireless Bridge** to start the local server.

2. **Scan the QR Code**:
   A QR code displays on screen containing a unique URL.
   Open your mobile camera or QR reader and scan the code.

3. **Capture Images**:
   The mobile web interface opens.
   Tap the camera button to take a photo of your coin.
   Review the preview and confirm or retake.

4. **Transfer Images**:
   Tap **Send to Archive** to transfer the image.
   The image appears in the Plate Editor on your desktop.

5. **Assign Image Type**:
   Click the image in the Plate Editor.
   Select **Obverse**, **Reverse**, or **Edge** to categorize it.
   The first image assigned becomes the primary image for the gallery.

6. **Stop the Session**:
   Click **Stop Bridge** when finished.
   The server closes and your mobile device disconnects.

## Supported Image Formats

- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PNG (.png)
- WebP (.webp)

Maximum file size: 10 MB per image.

## Troubleshooting

- **QR Code Not Scanning**: Ensure your mobile is on the same Wi-Fi network as the desktop
- **Upload Failed**: Check that the image is under 10 MB and in a supported format
- **Server Already Running**: Click **Stop Bridge** first, then restart
- **Network Timeout**: Move closer to your Wi-Fi router
